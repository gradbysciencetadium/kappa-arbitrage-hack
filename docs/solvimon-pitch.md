# Kappa Arbitrage — Solvimon Pitch ("Most likely to be a successful business")

> One-liner: **Kappa Arbitrage is feasibility-grade, data-backed strategic analysis for SMBs —
> at 1/10th the price of a consultant and delivered in minutes.** We start with UK childcare,
> where councils *require* a feasibility study before you can open.

---

## 1. The problem (a real, forced market)
- UK SMBs face high-stakes, expensive-if-wrong decisions (where to open, whether to expand) with
  **no data and no affordable help**. A formal feasibility study costs **£5,000–10,000 and takes
  60–90 days**; the alternative is gut feel.
- In our beachhead — **children's day nurseries** — this isn't optional: **local councils tell new
  operators to produce a feasibility study**, and planning applications demand demand-evidence.
  Forced demand, done badly today (chains pay for CACI licences; independents can't).

## 2. The product (working today, on real data)
- **Kappy** runs a structured intake conversation → a clean Context Brief.
- **Bara** runs a multi-step analysis grounded in **real UK government data** (Ofsted register,
  ONS Census 0–4 population, IMD deprivation) and **computes every number deterministically** —
  no fabricated figures — then cross-checks against a comparable and writes a cited report with a
  ranked recommendation and an implementation roadmap.
- **Live demo:** "Where in Croydon should I open my second nursery?" → ranked wards
  (New Addington / West Thornton / Broad Green) from 661 real providers + 28 wards of real Census data.

## 3. Why we win — the moat (what can't be copied)
The AI and the prompts are commodity. Our defensible assets compound with use:
1. **Validation / backtest library** — we log a blind prediction vs. the real outcome for known
   openings (publicly trackable via Ofsted). "Our models land within X% across N openings" is a
   sales weapon no generic AI tool can show.
2. **Proprietary SMB data** — every intake is data competitors don't have.
3. **Encoded method** — the analytical heuristics from real expert case studies, not a generic prompt.
4. **Sovereign, auditable AI** — UK gov data analysed via UK-sovereign-aligned inference (FLock),
   a real trust differentiator for a regulated buyer.
